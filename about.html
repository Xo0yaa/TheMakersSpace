OUR STORY

The Maker's Space was founded in September 2025 by John Feliciano S. Mallari. It began as a blog with DIY Tutorials, but has evolved into a global community for creative individuals. The goal of the organization is to provide a place—where people can explore their creativity, share their passion, and find support, regardless of their skill.

WHAT WE DO

The Maker's Space is a resource for DIY and crafting. It offers detailed tutorials, video guides, a community project gallery, and forum for—connecting with other makers. The platform covers various topics including home decor, upcycling, seasonal crafts, kid's activities. It aims to help people experience the joy and satisfaction of creating things with their own hands.

MISSION AND VISION

Inspire Creativity

The Maker's Space aims to inspire and guide individual to discover and unleash their creative potential.

Build Community

To build a welcoming community that encourages members to share their creations, knowledge, and successes with othe another.
